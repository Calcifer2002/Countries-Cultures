Introduction

Brazil is officially called the Federal Republic of Brazil and the capital is Brasilia. Its landmass has the mountains such as Serra do Mar, Serra do Espinhaço and many rivers such as Amazon, São Francisco, Paraná, The Tocantins. Brazil is the 5th populated country in the list of most populated countries. Rio de Janerio is a very important landmark of the country. Despite its beauty and resources its economic state is in shambles. Brazil accounts for over one-third of the Latin American population in the world. Sao Paulo is the country's economic and demographic center. Southeastern Brazil is only 11 percent of the country's landmass but 43 percent of the population lives there. Around 20 million people live in the Amazon basin alone.
