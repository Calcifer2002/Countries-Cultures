Introduction

In this section of the website we will be taking a look at Canada and Canadian culture. Located in northern North America, the country extends from the Atlantic Ocean in the east to the Pacific Ocean in the west and northward into the Arctic Ocean. With almost 10 million km2 of land and a population of 37 million, Canada is the second-largest country in the world. The country is governed by a constitutional monarchy, a federal structure, and parliamentary democracy. The queen of the United Kingdom is the head of state, and the prime minister of Canada is the head of government.
There are ten provinces and three territories in Canada. These subdivisions vary widely in both land and water area. The largest subdivision by land is Nunavut. The largest subdivision by water area is Quebec.
