Introduction

Pakistan is located in South Asia bordered by India, China, Iran, and Afghanistan. Pakistan has 4 major provinces: Sindh, Punjab, KPK, and Balochistan. Including this, it has federally administered regions called: Islamabad(Capital), Gilgit-Baltistan, and Azad Kashmir. The flag of Pakistan consists of white and green colours with a moon and star. White represents the minority non-muslims while green represents the majority Muslims. The moon star represents the state religion which is Islam. Pakistan comprises many ethnic groups within its society. Pashtuns are the second largest ethnic population after Punjabis in the country. We are going to talk about Pashtun culture, its people, its language, their religion, and their existence in Pakistan's society.
