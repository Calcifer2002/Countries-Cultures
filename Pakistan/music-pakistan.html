Music of Pakistan

Pashto music and songs are quite influenced by the Pashtuns people itself in terms of culture, tradition and poetry. Famous poets like Ghani Khan and Rehman Baba, their poetry have been transformed into classical music by singers like Sardar Takkar. The music style is mainly of two genres: classical and folktales. The traditional way of music consists of only one instrument called Tappay or Rabab. However, the modern music produced is the mixture of both the traditional and modern type of music. The lyrics of most of the songs are based on the idea of love, philosophy and on the conflicts that northern Pakistan have suffered through.
List of some Pashto music:

  • Jannat-u-Dunya
  • Oba derta Raoram
  • Qarar
  • ranra
  • Pakhwa
